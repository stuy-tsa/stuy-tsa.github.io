Why should I join?

Membership in TSA comes with opportunities to compete in local, state, and national tournaments, make friends across the country, learn more about careers in STEM, develop skills that will help you in the future, learn about yourself and what you want to pursue as a career, enter in a number of sponsored competitions for a chance to win amazing scholarships, honor society membership, and access to leadership training conferences.

About TSA

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national student organization devoted exclusively to the needs of students engaged in science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM). Open to students enrolled in or who have completed technology education courses, TSA’s membership includes over 200,000 middle and high school students in approximately 2,000 schools spanning 49 states.
